More info about service

At Rakestraw, Ltd. we have the ability to perform a broad range of professional engineering services. We have the capabilities to provide services in the pre-project, conceptual, detail, and implementation phases of your project.

More services we offer

  • Full understanding of ASME, ANSI, ASTM, API, TIA, SAE, ASCE, ISO, NACE, DEP, and other industry standards.
  • Research capabilities for industry standards and custom criteria and requirements.
  • Feasibility analyses and front-end conceptual engineering (FEED).
  • Hazard and operability studies (HAZOP).
  • Preliminary and Detailed engineering design and execution.
  • Project implementation through completion and maintenance.

Process

We follow widely accepted engineering processes to ensure our services meet project requirements and to provide a valuable resource for our clients.
