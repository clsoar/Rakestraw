Project Management

Managing your risk for turn-key value.

Contact Us

At Rakestraw, Ltd. we have the professionals available to manage your project from initiation and planning through execution and to provide continuing monitoring and control.

More services we offer

  • Initiation services such as drafting project proposal, work breakdown development, project scheduling, developing requirements, and budgeting
  • Planning services such as developing scope, selecting execution teams, resource allocation, risk management, developing quality assurance measures, and obtaining formal approvals
  • Execution services such as procurement, tracking, managing change orders and project modifications accordingly
  • Measuring project performance and performing project execution monitoring
  • Project closeout and punchlist completion

Process

We follow widely accepted project management processes to ensure our services meet customer requirements and to provide a valuable service for our clients.
